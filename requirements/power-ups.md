## Power-ups

These are positive effects to the player granted by collecting an "atom". Each power-up has a name, how likely it is to be awarded, and and an effect. The effecrts of power-ups can be stacked and combined.

Freuency, from most common to least-common: Common, Uncommon, Very Uncommon, Rare

# Rapid-fire increase

Name: "Rapid-fire increase"
Effect: The amount of time between bullets from being shot is reduced
Frequency: Common

# Triple-shot

Name: "Triple-shot"
Effect: When bullets are fired, two extra bullets get shot at 20-degree angles from the original bullet. This creates an effect where three bullets get shot at once.
Frequency: Very Uncommon

# Bullet-size increase

Name: Bullet-size increase
Effect: Bullets slightly scale up so they are slightly larger.
Frequency: Uncommon

# Rear shot

Name: Rear shot
Effect: In addition to the bullets firing forward, a single bullet also fires in the opposite, reverse direction.
Frequency: Uncommon

# Bullet Speedup

Name: Bullet speedup
Effect: The speed bullets travel gets slightly increased
Frequency: Common

# Black hole

Name: Black hole
Effect: The screen blinks white and an 8-bit wooshing sound plays. When the screen finishes blinking all destroyable objects get destroied.
Frequency: Rare

# Turbo

Name: Turbo
Effect: The player's rotate and move speed slightly increases
Frequency: Very Uncommon
